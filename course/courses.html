ECEN 248: Digital Systems Design

  • Boolean Algebra, AND, OR, NOT Gates. SOP, POS, NAND, NOR
  • K-Maps, logical minimalization, don't cares
  • Unsigned, signed numbers, number systems, addition, subtraction
  • Fast adders, fixed, float, ASCII

  • SR, D, Master-slave, T, JK flip-flops, shift-registers, counters
  • NMOS, CMOS, negative logic, PLA, PAL, FPGA
