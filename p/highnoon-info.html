Inspired by McCree's memorable ultimate quote in Blizzard's Overwatch, I created this site to let you know approximately when solar noon time will be in your location. It's not a great estimate, and I'm open to improvements. It's also one of the first real web pages I have made. I pulled the game files for the audio quotes, and looked up a font that looked similar to the game's. The website used for the solar noon approximation is here, and I found this to be most simple and least mathematically intensive (I am only a computer engineering major, after all). The rest of it was just guessing and checking Overwatch colors and eating cookies.
